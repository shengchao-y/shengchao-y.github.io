Shengchao Yan

I'm a PostDoc at Neurorobotics Lab, University of Freiburg. I did my Ph.D. at University of Freiburg, advised by Professor Wolfram Burgard. Prior to that, I received an M.Sc from the Karlsruhe Institue of Technology, advised by Professor Christoph Stiller. My work lies in the intersection between robotics and machine learning, with a focus on reinforcement learning for control of high DoF systems.
